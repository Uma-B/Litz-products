Click4 Water

click4water is a platform that can connect & bring the filtered as well as mineral water resources from the neighborhood to the doorstep of your resident ensuring highest water quality, affordable price & most important – timely service.

Features & Overviews

MAINTENANCE OF CLIENT & EMPLOYEE DETAILS ON CLOUD

All the client & employee details are stored in out private cloud which makes so easy for the Agency to access the their details anywhere


ONLINE BOOKING SERVICE.

Customer can book the water with a single click





TRACK ORDER

Both the Customers and Agency can track the orders and can guess the on time delivery

PAYMENT & PURCHASE HISTORY

The payment and purchase history can be easily viewed and tracked at any time




Agency

Agency provides water cans service on behalf of another business, group. We will provide separate login for Agency. Who are all employed in distributing the water can and what is the payment that are collected can be stored in this application. The payment and purchase history can be easily generated as reports through this.

  • Easily trackable

  • Payment history generation

  • Purchase history

  • Dynamic report generation.

EMPLOYEE

Employees are those who get the water can from the agency and distribute the water can to the end user. We will provide the employee module which consists of the details of a particular employee. All the payments that are made are recorded. So the payment collection history are generated as reports.

  • Take orders via online

  • Can purchase history generation

  • Collection amount details maintenance



CUSTOMERS

Customers are the end users who can book the water can through the help of internet. Each and every purchase and payment are recorded. So the reports can be viewed independently. Can look up the offres and make the purchase on time.

  • Book water can via internet

  • Reports
