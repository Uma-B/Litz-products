G-Tax

With the G-Tax on the radar, instead of undergoing time consuming filing of summarized returns, all you need to do now is submit the details of all your sales and purchase invoices once every month.To simplify the GST filing process, GTAX which enables taxpayers to file GST returns through our ERP software. Through GTAX all taxpayers will be able to calculate their returns online.GTAX has been upgraded and recognized to provide organizations and consultants an end-to-end solution to manage purchase, sale and G-Tax filing.

Features & Overviews

Process Control

G-Tax will assume the holistic responsibility to calculate the actual G-Tax returns based on the sale and purchase entries made in the ERP software.


Audit

G-Tax will calculate the returns as per your sale and purchase data and audit the same before filing G-Tax returns. Save your time and effort for other growth processes!





DATA Protection and Privacy

G-Tax will protect your sensitive sale/purchase data and use it for calculating, and filing the G-Tax Returns. No more tedious calculations!

DATA Archiving

G-Tax will archive and store all your data for multiple years. Have all your information, at one place - organized efficiently!
